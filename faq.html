Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our event planning services

Have questions about our event planning services? Browse through our most frequently asked questions below. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly!

What types of events do you organize?

We specialize in a wide range of events including weddings, corporate events, birthday parties, award ceremonies, product launches, cultural events, college festivals, and private parties. Each event is customized to meet your specific needs and vision.

Where are you located and what areas do you serve?

We are based in Muzaffarpur, Bihar, India. While we primarily serve Muzaffarpur and surrounding areas in Bihar, we also organize events across India. Contact us to discuss your event location and we'll be happy to accommodate your needs.

How experienced is your team?

AR Event and Wedding Planner has been in the event management industry since 2013, bringing over 10 years of experience. We have successfully organized 150+ events and served 100+ satisfied clients, making us one of the most trusted event planners in Muzaffarpur.

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large corporate events to ensure availability and adequate planning time. For smaller events, 1-2 months notice is usually sufficient. However, we can accommodate last-minute requests based on our availability.

What is your booking process?

Our booking process is simple: 1) Contact us via phone, email, or our website. 2) Schedule a consultation to discuss your event requirements. 3) Receive a customized proposal and quote. 4) Sign the agreement and pay the booking advance. 5) Start planning your dream event with our team!

Can I make changes after booking?

Yes, we understand that plans can change. We're flexible and can accommodate modifications to your event details. However, significant changes close to the event date may incur additional charges. We recommend finalizing major details at least 2-4 weeks before the event.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies based on the type of event, number of guests, services required, and event duration. We offer customized packages to fit different budgets. Visit our Pricing page for more details or contact us for a personalized quote.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. Typically, we require a booking advance (30-40%), followed by installments leading up to the event, with the final payment due before or on the event day. We can customize payment schedules based on your needs.

Are there any hidden costs?

No, we believe in complete transparency. All costs are clearly outlined in our proposal and contract. Any additional services requested after booking will be discussed and approved by you before implementation. We provide detailed breakdowns of all expenses.

Do you provide decoration services?

Yes! We provide comprehensive decoration services including stage setup, floral arrangements, lighting, backdrops, centerpieces, and themed decorations. Our creative team works with you to bring your vision to life with stunning decor that matches your event theme.

Can you help with venue selection?

Absolutely! We have partnerships with various venues across Muzaffarpur and Bihar. We can help you find the perfect venue based on your event type, guest count, budget, and preferences. We handle all venue coordination and logistics as well.

Do you provide catering services?

Yes, we work with trusted catering partners who can provide a wide range of cuisines and menu options. From traditional Indian cuisine to international dishes, we ensure high-quality food service that delights your guests. We can also accommodate dietary restrictions and preferences.

Do you offer photography and videography?

Yes, we collaborate with professional photographers and videographers who specialize in event coverage. We can arrange for photo and video services, including candid photography, cinematic videos, drone shots, and photo booths to capture all your special moments.

Can you arrange entertainment for events?

Definitely! We can arrange various entertainment options including DJs, live bands, dancers, anchors/emcees, magicians, and other performers. We tailor entertainment to match your event theme and audience preferences.

What makes AR Event and Wedding Planner different?

Our commitment to excellence, creativity, and personalized service sets us apart. We focus on understanding your unique vision and bringing it to life with meticulous attention to detail. With over 10 years of experience, a dedicated team, and a track record of 150+ successful events, we ensure your event is unforgettable.
