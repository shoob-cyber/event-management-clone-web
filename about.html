About AR event and wedding planner

At AR event and wedding planner, we believe every event is an opportunity to craft unforgettable memories. Based at Christ University, Kengeri Campus, we specialize in creating exceptional experiences — from vibrant festivals to elegant private gatherings. With creativity, precision, and a passion for perfection, our team transforms your vision into reality.

Our services cover end-to-end event planning including concept design, logistics management, venue coordination, entertainment booking, and post-event support. Whether it's a corporate event, a private celebration, or a cultural gathering, we ensure every detail is meticulously managed.

We take pride in our customer-first approach, dedicated project management, innovative ideas, and seamless execution. AR Event Management is your trusted partner for turning ordinary moments into extraordinary memories.
